🤖 Automated Paper Trades —
SELL Setup B (post-cross): Spot crosses below a strike. If PE unwind ≥ 3L at the moment of crossing → SELL immediately. Otherwise wait for a retracement (bullish candle), then PE unwind ≥ 3L on the next falling candle → SELL, only if spot ≥ 40 pts above the next lower strike. SL = entry + 20 pts.
SELL Setup C (reversal candle breakdown): After a falling candle, if the next candle is bullish (bounce) and ATM put OI unwinds ≥ 2L on that bounce candle → the bounce is just short-covering. Arm a SELL. Entry when any subsequent candle breaks below the reversal candle's low. Target = next lower 50-pt strike. SL = reversal candle's high + 30 pts.
BUY Setup B (post-cross): Spot crosses above a strike. CE unwind ≥ 3L at cross → BUY immediately. Otherwise wait for a retracement (bearish candle), then CE unwind ≥ 3L on the next rising candle → BUY, ≥ 40 pts gap to next higher strike. SL = entry − 20 pts.
BUY Setup C (reversal candle breakout): After a rising candle, bearish reversal candle with ATM CE unwind ≥ 2L → arm a BUY. Entry when price breaks above the reversal candle's high. Target = next higher strike. SL = reversal candle's low − 30 pts.
